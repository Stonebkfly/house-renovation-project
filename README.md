## Project Structure

### 01-Planning
Contains initial project planning documents, scope definition, and room-by-room renovation plans.

### 02-Budget
Budget tracking spreadsheets, cost estimates, and expense records.

### 03-Contractors
Contact information, bids, contracts, and communication logs with contractors and vendors.

### 04-Permits-and-Legal
Building permits, inspection reports, HOA approvals, and legal documents.

### 05-Design-and-Inspiration
Design plans, color schemes, mood boards, and inspiration images.

### 06-Materials-and-Shopping
Shopping lists, material specifications, product links, and purchase tracking.

### 07-Timeline-and-Schedule
Project timeline, milestone tracking, and scheduling documents.

### 08-Documentation
- **Before-Photos:** Photos taken before renovation starts
- **After-Photos:** Photos taken after completion
- **Receipts:** All purchase receipts
- **Contracts:** Signed contracts with contractors

### 09-Progress-Photos
Weekly progress photos organized chronologically.

### 10-Warranties-and-Manuals
Product warranties, instruction manuals, and maintenance guides.

## Quick Start Guide

1. Fill out the **Project Overview** section above
2. Complete the **Project-Plan.md** in the 01-Planning folder
3. Set up your budget in **Budget-Tracker.md** (02-Budget folder)
4. Create your project timeline in **Timeline.md** (07-Timeline-and-Schedule folder)
5. Document contractors in **Contractor-List.md** (03-Contractors folder)
6. Take before photos and store them in 08-Documentation/Before-Photos

## Important Reminders

- Take photos before, during, and after each phase
- Keep all receipts in 08-Documentation/Receipts
- Document all change orders
- Update budget regularly
- Maintain communication logs with contractors
- Store all warranties and manuals for future reference
